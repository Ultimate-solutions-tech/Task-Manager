Welcome to the Task Management System

Our Task Management System is designed to help you stay organized and productive. Whether you're managing personal tasks or coordinating with a team, our system provides the tools you need to keep track of your tasks and prioritize your work effectively.

Features

  • User Registration and Authentication: Secure registration and login system to protect your data.
  • Task Management: Create, update, and delete tasks with attributes such as title, description, deadline, and priority.
  • Task Filtering: Filter tasks based on priority or due date to find what you need quickly.
  • Search Functionality: Easily search for tasks by keywords in the title or description.
  • Task Board: Visualize your tasks in segments: To Do, Doing, and Done for better workflow management.
